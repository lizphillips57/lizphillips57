TV Head

Jun - Oct 2021

~1 ft x 1 ft x 1.5ft

A CRT television with all inner components removed, and screen replaced with an LED array.

The array was hand-soldered and is controlled via an Adafruit Circuit Playground microcontroller. The screen can display different animations and react to sound. The detachable "cat ears" were 3D modeled and printed, and house two Bluetooth speakers that can play stereo audio.

The entire TV is designed to be portable and worn like a helmet, with the bulk of the weight being carried on the shoulders. The screen is transluscent from the inside, so that the wearer can see through the array.
