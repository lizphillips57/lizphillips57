Small Worlds

July 2023

~3 ft x 2 ft

An interactive audio-visual projection mapping created for a Media Arts course.

Taking inspiration from the abstract geometric shapes of Kandinsky's "Small Worlds IV", each component of the 3D-printed sculpture reacts to sound and music in a different way. The colors, shapes, and textures move with the rhythm, with some parts representing bass frequencies and others representing high frequencies.

The 3D printed components were modeled in Fusion 360. The live visuals are generated using TouchDesigner. The projection mapping is done through MadMapper.
